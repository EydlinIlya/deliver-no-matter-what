How we calculate

A warning or alert signal for your area starts a shelter session. The session ends on a safety signal, or auto-closes 10 min after the last alert if no further alerts arrive within 45 min. Sessions crossing time window boundaries are clipped, not dropped. Contributions are your public GitHub activity in the last 30 days.
